Thank you for buying the Fantasy House Kit.
This Kit contains everything you need to build your very own fantasy houses.
Every texture included is hand painted and looks amazing in both ambient lit games and games that use shadows. These textures give the houses you make with this kit that real fantasy flavor.
The construction pieces are usable via drag and drop from the prefabs folder. Also included in the prefabs folder are 3 example houses made with the construction pieces.
Hope you enjoy this asset pack.
